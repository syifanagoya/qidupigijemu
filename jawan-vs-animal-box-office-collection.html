Day Wise Comparison of Shahrukh Khan vs Ranbir Kapoor Starrer

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Tuesday, August 27, 2024

Jawan vs Animal box office collection: The year 2023 has been a blockbuster year for Bollywood, with two of the biggest stars, Shahrukh Khan and Ranbir Kapoor, clashing at the box office with their respective films, Jawan and Animal. Both films are action thrillers with high-octane sequences, gripping plots, and stellar performances. But which film has the edge over the other in terms of box office collections, ratings, and audience response? Let’s find out.

Shahrukh Khan’s Jawan vs Ranbir Kapoor’s Animal Movie

Jawan, directed by Atlee and produced by Gauri Khan, was released on September 7, 2023, on the occasion of Ganesh Chaturthi. The film stars Shahrukh Khan as Vikram Rathore, a jailer who moonlights as a vigilante, along with Nayanthara, Vijay Sethupathi, Deepika Padukone, Priyamani, and Sanya Malhotra in pivotal roles. The film received positive reviews from critics and audiences alike, and opened to a massive collection of Rs 64.23 crore on its first day, making it the highest opening day collection of 2023.

The film maintained its momentum over the weekend and the weekdays and crossed the Rs 200 crore mark in just six days. As of December 03, 2023, the film has collected Rs 587 crore in India and Rs 1,021 crore worldwide, making it the highest-grossing Hindi film of 2023 and the fourth-highest-grossing Indian film of all time.

Animal, directed by Sandeep Reddy Vanga and produced by T-Series, was released on December 1, 2023, on the occasion of World AIDS Day. The film stars Ranbir Kapoor as Raghav, a ruthless gangster who is obsessed with his ex-girlfriend, along with Anil Kapoor, Bobby Deol, Parineeti Chopra, and Prem Chopra in key roles. The film received mixed reviews from critics but was praised for its performances, especially Ranbir Kapoor’s.

The film opened to a record-breaking collection of Rs 72.15 crore on its first day, surpassing Jawan’s opening day collection and becoming the highest opening day collection of all time. The film continued to dominate the box office over the weekend and the weekdays and crossed the Rs 200 crore mark in just four days. As of December 03, 2023, the film has collected Rs 360 crore in India and Rs 720 crore worldwide, making it the second-highest-grossing Hindi film of 2023 and the seventh-highest-grossing Indian film of all time.

Jawan vs Animal Box Office Collection

Here is a table of an overview of the day-wise comparison of Jawan vs Animal Box Office Collection:

Rating

Jawan has received an IMDb rating of 7.2 out of 10, based on 70,000 votes. The film has been praised for its action, story, direction, and performances, especially Shahrukh Khan’s. The film has also received positive reviews from critics, who have given it an average rating of 3.5 out of 5. The film has been appreciated for its social message, its portrayal of women’s empowerment, and its tribute to fatherhood.

Animal has received an IMDb rating of 8.6 out of 10, based on 3,700 votes. The film has been lauded for its performances, especially Ranbir Kapoor’s. The film has also received mixed reviews from critics, who have given it an average rating of 2.5 out of 5. The film has been criticized for its violence, its plot, and its direction. The film has been compared to Sandeep Reddy Vanga’s previous film, Kabir Singh, which was also a controversial and polarizing film.

Audience Response

Both Jawan and Animal have received a huge response from the audience, who have flocked to the theatres to watch their favorite stars in action. Both films have also generated a lot of buzz on social media, with fans and celebrities sharing their views and opinions on the films. Both films have also created several records and milestones at the box office, proving their mass appeal and star power.

Jawan has received a positive response from the audience, who have loved the film for its entertainment value, its message, and its star cast. The film has also received a lot of appreciation from celebrities, who have praised Shahrukh Khan for his comeback, Atlee for his direction, and the other actors for their performances. The film has also been hailed as a patriotic film, which celebrates the spirit of democracy and the power of the common man.

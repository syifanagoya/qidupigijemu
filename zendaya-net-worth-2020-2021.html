Early Life

Zendaya was born on the 1st of September in the year 1996 in the city of Oakland situated in California to parents Claire Marie and Kazambe Ajamu. Zendaya is known to have an African-American origin.

She was introduced to the theatre field by her mother who had served in California Shakespeare Theatre in the region of Orinda, where Zendaya was known to have performed for the first time. At the age of eight, Zendaya had been an active member of a hip-hop and hula dance group entitled Future Shock Oakland, where she had shone brightly.

She had received her education from the prestigious Oakland School for the Arts where she had also participated in several plays. Henceforth, Zendaya had shifted to the CalShakes Conservatory Program and “American Conservatory Theater”. Among the major plays, “Once On This Island”, “Caroline, Or Change”, “Twelfth Night”, “As You Like It” and “Richard III” remains her breakthrough roles.

Kelley Jakle Net Worth

Posted by Martina Birk on Tuesday, September 17, 2024

Kelley Jakle:

$3 million

Kelley Jakle is an American actress. She’s also a singer. She first appeared in the singing competition show The Sing-Off. In 2012, she made her film debut as Jessica Smith in Pitch Perfect. She has since reprised her role in the sequels Pitch Perfect 2 and Pitch Perfect 3, gaining her recognition. Other films include 42, The 420 Movie: Mary & Jane, and Single Miserable Human. She has also made guest appearances in the shows Workaholics, Adam DeVine’s House Party, Scorpion, Uncle Grandpa, and Resident Advisors. As a singer, she has released the EPs By the Way and Spare Change in 2007 and 2008, respectively.

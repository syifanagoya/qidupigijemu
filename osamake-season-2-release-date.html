Osamake Season 2 Release Date Status: Announced or Canceled?

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Friday, May 24, 2024

Osamake is a Japanese anime television series that is adapted from the light novel series of the name written by Shūichi Nimaru. The anime is written by Yoriko Tomita and directed by Takashi Naoya. Doga Kobo picked up the series for an anime television adaptation. 

The first season of the anime premiered on April 14, 2021, on At-X, Tokyo MX, Sun, and other networks, and the last episode premiered on June 30, 2021.

The series consisted of twelve episodes and it is licensed by Muse Communication in Southeast Asia and South Asia whereas, outside Asia, it is licensed by Crunchyroll.

It performed well and received positive reviews both from the critics as well as the audience and has an IMDB rating of 5.9 out of 10 and 6.31 out of 10 on MyAnimeList. 

After the success and popularity, the first season of anime gained, fans have been waiting for an announcement about the renewal of the anime for a second season, and here’s what we know so far about it.

Osamake Season 2 Release Date

It has been almost a year since the last episode of season one aired and there has been no official announcement made yet about the second season of the anime.

But in May last year, Kadokawa revealed that they will produce at least 40 anime projects every year by 2023 and the new Kadokawa light books and manga series will probably be incorporated in several of such projects and we can expect many more to approach in the future as well. 

We can expect the second season of the anime to premiere soon, perhaps by next year and we will be able to watch a brand new season of the anime.

Osamake Season 2 Characters

Though nothing has been shared about the characters who will be seen in the new season of the anime, we can expect most of the characters to return from the first season.

  • Sueharu Maru dubbed by Yoshihitsugu Matsuoka
  • Shirokusa Kachi dubbed by Ayane Sakura
  • Kuroha Shida dubbed by Inori Minase
  • Maria Momosaka dubbed by Saori nishi
  • Mitsuru Abe dubbed by Takuma Terashima
  • Tetsuhiko Kai dubbed by Nobunaga Shimazaki
  • Mitsuru Abe dubbed by Takuma Terashima
  • Meiko Mine dubbed by M.A.O

There may be certain changes or additions to the existing voice cast but the announcement is yet to be made.

Osamake Season 2 Expected Storyline

Throughout her high school, Sueharu Maru didn’t have a partner. His childhood mate Kuroha Shida, a small and cute Onee-san-type young lady who has an easy-going personality, resides nearby.

Sueharu has been extremely interested in Kuroha’s first love, Shirokusa Kachi, a great symbol and an award-winning creator at school, however, Sueharu dumps him.

Due to Shirokusa’s indifference, Sueharu thinks he has a shot at becoming a student body leader. Sueharu is shattered when he discovers that Shirokusa has a boyfriend when he ultimately confesses his feelings.

Sueharu is followed by Kuroha, who claims to be capable of helping him take revenge on Shirokusa and her boyfriend.

The storyline for the second season is yet to be known.

We will keep you updated as there is any official announcement about Osamake Season 2 until then stay tuned to our website.

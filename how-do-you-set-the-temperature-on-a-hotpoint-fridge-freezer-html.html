How do you set the temperature on a Hotpoint fridge freezer?

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Tuesday, March 26, 2024

Answer

If your refrigerator has a dial control, you may alter the temperature in single increments by turning the dial in small increments. The numbers on the dial are numbered “0” through “9,” with “9” representing the coldest temperature. To modify the freezer temperature, turn the freezer dial in single increments by turning it clockwise.

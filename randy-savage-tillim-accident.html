Randy Savage Tillim Accident

Posted by Martina Birk on Thursday, February 8, 2024

Randy Savage faced a garage accident in Seminole, Florida, with his Jeep Wrangler whiles driving his wife. The unfortunate incident made Randy Savage unresponsive for some time and later caused his death.

Prior to the incident, it is reported that on the fateful morning of May 20, 2011, as Randy Savage was driving, he had a sudden heart attack that made his loose control of his driving and crashed his car into a nearby tree.

Randy Savage’s autopsy reports showed that his death was not much associated with the accident, but he had an undetected atherosclerotic heart disease that caused his heart attack.

Randy Savage, known in real life as Randall Mario Poffo, was an American professional wrestler and professional baseball player. Randy Savage was an inductee into the Wrestling Observer Newsletter Hall of Fame and WWE Hall of Fame.

Randy Savage played baseball as a catcher for the likes of the Saint Louis Cardinals, the Cincinnati Reds, and the Tampa Tarpons. Randy Savage was an alumnus of Southern Illinois University–Carbondale.
